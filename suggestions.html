Beauty Picks

Curated suggestions for your next glow-up.

Must-Have

Sunscreen under PinkFlash Palette

To make those pink shimmers pop, always prep with a hydrating sunscreen base. We suggest the Originote Sunscreen for a non-sticky finish.

Technique

The "Gradient" Lip Tip

Apply your BLK Lipstick only to the center of your lips and blend outwards with a finger for a soft, dreamy look.

Combo

Dewy Cheek Hack

Mix a tiny drop of the Originote Moisturizer with your BLK blush for a sheer, glass-skin effect.
